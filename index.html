Who We Are

Experience Farm Life, Support Conservation

Step into The Fluffy Cow Cafe in Ocala, Florida, and enjoy unique farm animal encounters that directly support Noah's Ark Sanctuary.

Our cafe provides a joyful and educational experience with our beloved Highland Cows and their diverse animal friends. Each visit helps us further our mission to care for and protect animals in need.

The idea for The Fluffy Cow Cafe came from our desire to bring conservation awareness to the community in a fun, engaging way. We believe that meaningful interactions with animals are key to fostering understanding and empathy for all creatures ‚Äî especially our gentle, fluffy bovine friends!
